Agriwise is an intelligent recommendation system designed to optimize crop nutrition management for farmers and agricultural professionals. By leveraging soil data, crop requirements, and environmental factors, It provides precise fertilizer recommendations tailored to specific field conditions and weather conditions. This system aims to enhance crop yield improve soil health, and promote sustainable agricultural practices as a result it leads to profits and for that our system has also been designed to calculate profit estimation too. Through data-driven insights and user-friendly interfaces, It empowers users to make informed decisions, reduce input costs, and contribute to more efficient and ecofriendly farming.
